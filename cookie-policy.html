Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how ISECT Welfare and Education Society ("we", "us", or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies on our website.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your web browser. They help us improve your experience and understand how our website is used.

How We Use Cookies

  • To remember your preferences and settings.
  • To analyze website traffic and usage patterns.
  • To enhance website functionality.

Managing Cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect your experience on our website.
