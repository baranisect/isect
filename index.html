About Us

Who We Are

ISECT Welfare and Education Society is a reputed organization in Baran, Rajasthan, working in challenging and marginalized areas to uplift the community. We empower youth and marginalized communities through skill development, digital literacy, and social transformation.

  • Reg. No.: 51/BARAN/2008-09
  • Focus: Skill development, digital literacy, women empowerment, and social awareness
  • ⁠Affiliated with:
    1. RSLDC (Rajasthan Skill Livelihood Development Corporation- A Government of Rajasthan Undertaking)
    2. MoRD (Ministry of Rural Development)

Our Founder

ISECT Welfare and Education Society was founded in 2008 by Mr. Sabir Ahmad with the vision to nurture the unique skills of every youth and promote social transformation through education and service.

Our Vision

“HUNAR HAI TO KYA FIKAR HAI” – Focusing on skill-based, self-sustainable development for all individuals. Every initiative at ISECT is rooted in this core value.

Our Mission

To provide awareness and access to government-funded programs, combining traditional and modern methods to improve life outcomes for beneficiaries and empower marginalized communities.
