About Us

ISECT Welfare and Education Society was founded in 2008 by Mr. Sabir Ahmad with the vision to nurture the unique skills of every youth and promote social transformation through education and service. We are a reputed organization in Baran, Rajasthan, working in challenging and marginalized areas to uplift the community.

Vision: "HUNAR HAI TO KYA FIKAR HAI" – Focusing on skill-based, self-sustainable development for all.

Mission: To provide awareness and access to government-funded programs, combining traditional and modern methods to improve life outcomes for beneficiaries.
