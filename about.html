About Us

ISECT Welfare and Education Society, Baran, was founded in 2008 by Mr. Sabir Ahmad. Our mission is to empower youth and marginalized communities through skill development, digital literacy, and social transformation. We are committed to providing government-sponsored programs and awareness campaigns to uplift society.

Vision

“HUNAR HAI TO KYA FIKAR HAI” – We focus on skill-based, self-sustainable development for all individuals.

Mission

To provide awareness about government-funded programs and appeal to all beneficiaries with different special skill sets. We combine traditional methods with modern adaptations to provide stimulating programs for beneficiaries, enabling them to enjoy improved life outcomes as productive members of society.

Our Work

  • Skill development and digital literacy programs
  • Women empowerment and social awareness initiatives
  • Community campaigns: HIV/AIDS, human rights, self-help groups, and more
  • Affiliated with state and central government schemes
